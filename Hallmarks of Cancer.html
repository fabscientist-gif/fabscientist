Hallmarks of Cancer — Revisiting Hanahan and Weinberg in 2025

Published on July 2025 • Science Explained

Introduction

Cancer is not a single disease but a diverse collection of disorders that arise when normal cells gradually acquire uncontrolled growth.

In 2000, Douglas Hanahan and Robert Weinberg introduced the Hallmarks of Cancer framework.

"The Hallmarks of Cancer provide a common language for understanding complex disease."

The Hallmarks of Cancer

  • Sustaining proliferative signaling
  • Evading growth suppressors
  • Resisting cell death
  • Enabling immortality
  • Angiogenesis
  • Metastasis
  • Metabolic reprogramming
  • Immune evasion

Conclusion

The Hallmarks of Cancer remains a foundational framework in biology.
